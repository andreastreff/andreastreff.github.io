My Background

I work as a consultant supporting businesses large and small, for profit and non-profit with Tech SEO, Analytics and Conversion Rate Optimization at Elementive. I love what I do. Each day I get the privilege to work with businesses of all shapes and sizes with missions ranging from improving the lives of children to helping people plan their dream vacations to connecting businesses with the information, workforce and skills they need to succeed.

While each client is widely varied in their mission, they all have a similar goal of delivering an extraordinary experience and product to their customers. We get to help our clients achieve that goal by improving the technical performance of their websites (like speed, errors and mobile), setting up custom configurations of Google Analytics and evaluating customer conversion paths.
